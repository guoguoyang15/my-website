My Passion

As one of the most common pets today, cats are treated as part of the family members by countless pet lovers and play a vital role in a harmonious family atmosphere. The concept of having a cat as a young person's first pet is becoming more and more popular throughout the world. After all, cats can take care of themselves during the time their owners are away, even if the owner is away for half a month, because they can stand to be lonely. And when the owner comes home, this cute little pet can be the first to give the most affectionate greetings to its adopter and share the day's joys and sorrows together, there is nothing better than that, right? As a cat slave and a new cat owner, I am happy to share some photos of my pet cat "Sydney", a purebred American Shorthair, who has just celebrated her first birthday recently. In addition, in order to make the website more meaningful and to reflect the sense of responsibility of cat owners, I will also update from time to time some of the problems that new cat owners are likely to encounter, as well as the relevant solutions, with relevant pictures.
